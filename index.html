How to Play!

  1. The timer will start when the player chooses and clicks to cards to turn over
  2. If the pictures match, the cards will remain turned over on the board
  3. If the pictures dont match, they will be returned face down
  4. Once all the cards are turned over the game will end
  5. **Remember** You are working against the clock, to get on the leaderboard, you must be quick!
  6. Good luck
