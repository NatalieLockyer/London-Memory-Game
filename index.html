How to Play!


  1. The timer will start when you select your first two cards
  2. If the pictures match, the cards will remain turned over on the board
  3. If the pictures dont match, they will be returned face down
  4. Once all the cards match the game will end
  5. *Remember* You are working against the clock, check out your time and total moves & play again to see if you can beat it.
  6. Good luck
