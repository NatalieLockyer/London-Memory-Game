How to Play!

  1. Player chooses two cards to click and turn over
  2. If the pictures match, the cards will remain turned over on the board
  3. If the pictures dont match, they will return face down
  4. Once all the cards are turned over the game will end
  5. **But Remember** You are working against the clock, if the time runs out, you lose!
  6. Good luck
